Represents the individual or entity that uses the telecommunications services. Contains personal information, service usage history, and subscription details. ^bhCCe3mM

Service:
Defines the telecommunications services offered, such as mobile data, voice, internet, or bundled services. Includes service type, features, and pricing. ^sljdtrEl

Subscription:
Tracks the active services that a customer is subscribed to, including the start date, end date, and service status (active, inactive, etc.). ^6sToYKam

Churn:
Represents the event when a customer stops using the services. Includes churn date, reason for churn (if available), and churn risk score (predicted). ^46zqtdv6

Usage Data:
Captures the historical usage of telecommunications services by the customer, such as data consumption, call durations, SMS usage, and other metrics that reflect service utilization. ^XJ40gCe1

Billing Information:
Stores billing and payment history for each customer, including invoices, payment dates, amounts, and payment methods. ^SCFy4Dmb

Demographic Data:
Stores customer demographic information such as age, gender, location, and other factors that may influence customer behavior. ^KCvvoiZJ

Retention Strategy:
Captures information about strategies or actions taken to retain customers, such as offers, discounts, or service upgrades provided to prevent churn. ^FT01qXxX

Churn Prediction Model:
Contains the model used to predict the likelihood of a customer churning. This includes the features used for prediction, model parameters, and the output (churn probability). ^w6fNQkve

Tracks interactions between customers and the service provider, including customer support calls, complaints, feedback, and any marketing campaigns targeted toward them. ^vVwUE3XS
